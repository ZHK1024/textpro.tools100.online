How Does Chinese Character Counting Work?

Our Chinese character counter uses Unicode character range detection to identify Chinese characters. The tool specifically looks for characters in the following Unicode ranges:

  • CJK Unified Ideographs (U+4E00-U+9FFF): The main block containing most commonly used Chinese characters
  • CJK Extension A (U+3400-U+4DBF): Additional Chinese characters and variants
  • CJK Extension B and beyond: Rare and historical Chinese characters
  • CJK Compatibility (U+F900-U+FAFF): Compatibility characters for legacy systems
